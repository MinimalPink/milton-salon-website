Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Milton Salon and Barber Shop ("we," "us," "our") operates this website to share information about our services, hours, location, and to help you book an appointment. This page explains what information this website collects, how it's used, and your choices.

Information we collect

This website itself does not have a contact form or account system, so we do not directly collect or store personal information you submit here. The site does use a small number of third-party services that may collect limited technical information automatically (such as browser type, device type, and general location) for the purposes described below.

Booking through Fresha

When you book an appointment, you're taken to Fresha, an independent third-party booking platform. Any information you provide to book an appointment (such as your name, phone number, or email) is collected and processed directly by Fresha under their own privacy policy, not by us. We recommend reviewing Fresha's Privacy Notice before booking.

Analytics and cookies

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use this site — for example, which pages are viewed and how visitors found us — so we can improve the site over time. These tools may use cookies or similar technology and may collect information such as your IP address and browsing behavior in an anonymized or aggregated form. We do not use this data to identify you personally, and we do not sell any data collected through this site.

Third-party links

This site links out to third-party platforms including Google Maps, Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your information, and we encourage you to review those policies separately.

Your choices

  • You can use your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time.
  • You are never required to use our online booking link — you're always welcome to call or walk in instead.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time as our tools or services change. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date above.
