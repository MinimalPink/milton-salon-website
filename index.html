FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins are always welcome. That said, booking ahead online guarantees your preferred time and barber, especially on weekends.

How do I book online?

Tap "Book Now" anywhere on this page to open our live booking calendar on Fresha. Pick your service, barber and time, and you'll get an instant confirmation.

What are your hours?

We're open seven days a week: Monday–Thursday 10 AM–7 PM, Friday 9:30 AM–7 PM, Saturday 9:30 AM–6 PM, and Sunday 10 AM–5 PM.

Is there parking?

Yes, free on-site parking is available directly in front of the shop at 3710 Old Milton Pkwy.

Do you take cards, or cash only?

We accept all major credit and debit cards as well as cash. Tips are appreciated but never required.

Can I request a specific barber?

Absolutely — you can choose your barber directly when booking online, or just ask for someone by name when you walk in.
